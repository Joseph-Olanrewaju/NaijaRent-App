Frequently Asked Questions

How does rent payment work? ▼
Tenants pay through NaijaRent's secure platform. We collect the full amount (rent + agent fees + platform fee), then disburse to landlords within 24 hours. You receive a payment reference for every transaction.
How do I verify my listing? ▼
Submit your listing and our admin team will review within 48 hours. Verified listings appear in tenant searches. We may request additional documents for expensive properties.
What are the agent fees? ▼
Regular agents set 10–20% fees. NaijaRent affiliate agents earn 10% automatically with 5% going to NaijaRent as platform fee, making a total 15% agent fee for those listings.
Is my NIN information safe? ▼
Absolutely. NIN data is encrypted and stored securely. It is only used for identity verification and is never shared with third parties without your consent.
How do I become a NaijaRent affiliate agent? ▼
Register on the Affiliate page with your NIN, valid ID, bank account, and personal details. Once approved, you can start listing vacant houses and earning 10% on every rent payment.
